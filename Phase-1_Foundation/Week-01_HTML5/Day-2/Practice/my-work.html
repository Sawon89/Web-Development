Why Do I Want to Be a Web Developer?

Date: July 06, 2026

The current era is completely digital, and web developers are the craftsmen behind this digital world. The process of bringing any idea to life on a screen through coding and making it usable for millions of people worldwide fascinates me the most. I truly enjoy the challenge of learning new technologies every day and using them to solve real-world problems.

Learning web development isn't just about writing code; it also strongly builds your logical thinking and problem-solving skills. The feeling you get when a website is built step-by-step from the front-end to the back-end and finally works live is truly unique. The opportunity to constantly update myself and create something innovative is my biggest inspiration for becoming a web developer.

In the future, I want to establish myself as a Full-Stack Web Engineer. I aim to build modern and user-friendly web applications that will make people's daily lives easier and more dynamic. This learning journey is long, but with every new line of code, I am getting closer to my goal.



What I Will Learn

  1. HTML5 & Semantic Structure
  2. CSS3, Flexbox & Grid
  3. JavaScript (ES6+)
  4. React.js (Front-end Framework)
  5. Node.js & Express.js (Back-end)
  6. Database Management (MongoDB/SQL)
  7. Version Control with Git & GitHub
